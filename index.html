About Me

Welcome to my page! I'm Susan Marshall and I'm a full-stack web developer. This site showcases some of my work and what I can do for you.

I graduated from Boston University with a Bachelor's Degree in Mathematics and computer Science, and spent nearly 30 years programming and doing technical support for SAS Institute. I enjoyed making web pages to share knowledge across our international offices and now I focus solely on web design.

When I'm not communing with my computer, I hang out with my cats (at their whim, not mine), knit (preferably without the cats' "help"), hike, and bike on the many gorgeous Raleigh greenway trails. I also like to travel - currently at 65 countries and counting!
